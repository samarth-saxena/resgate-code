Visual Question Answering

VQA is a multimodal task that involves understanding both visual and linguistic inputs and using that knowledge to generate a natural language answer. In a VQA task, a model is presented with an image and a question about the image in natural language. The model must then use its multimodal knowledge to generate a natural language answer.
